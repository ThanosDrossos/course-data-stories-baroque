**Abstract:** This data story explores Baroque ceiling paintings in Germany using metadata from the NFDI4Culture and CbDD knowledge graphs.
We integrate and normalize entities (paintings, painters, buildings, locations, and iconographic subjects) into a DuckDB-based analytical dataset.
Through spatial, temporal, and painter-centered analyses, we reveal patterns of production, themes, and artistic networks in Baroque ceiling painting.
